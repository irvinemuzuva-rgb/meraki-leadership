HQ Collective — Harare Queers Collective

Since 2019 I have organised and facilitated safe, brave spaces for the LGBTIQ+ community in Harare — places where identity is celebrated, where grief is witnessed, and where community-driven solutions to real challenges are born together. This work is close to my heart as a queer-identifying person who believes every human being deserves to be fully seen and fully safe.

The HQ Collective holds circles of honest conversation, capacity building, and joy — rooted in the conviction that liberation is a collective practice.

My Philosophy

The most powerful resource in any room is the wisdom already there


I have spent over a decade holding the conviction that people — when genuinely invited — are extraordinary. Not because they are special, but because each of us carries lived knowledge, latent creativity, and a deep longing to contribute to something that matters.

"People give their energy and lend their resources to what matters most to them — in work as in life."

My practice rests on this truth. I do not arrive with answers. I arrive with methodologies carefully shaped to open the space where your group's own intelligence can surface, self-organise, and take meaningful action.

I am proud to work alongside a diverse international community of practitioners, drawing on wisdom traditions and fieldwork spanning five continents.

Visual Practice

Turning conversations into living pictures

I am a graphic recorder and graphic facilitator — someone who translates the invisible currents of group dialogue into visible, memorable images in real time.

When words dissolve into the air, a well-placed drawing stays. It anchors insight, bridges language, and gives a group a shared mirror to see their own intelligence reflected back.

I offer graphic recording for conferences, strategic retreats, community dialogues, and any gathering where visual memory matters.

What I Offer

Three pathways to collective flourishing

Each engagement begins with deep listening — to you, your context, and the true need beneath your call. From there, I draw on a rich palette of methodologies to design a process that is strategically precise and humanly alive.

01

Leadership Capacity to Build Capacity

The most sustainable change comes from within. I develop the leadership skills, facilitation competencies, and relational intelligence that enable your people to host meaningful dialogue and build self-organising cultures — long after our time together ends.

For: Executive teams, facilitation corps, L&D, community leadership programmes

02

Organisational Innovation & Strategy

Visions written in boardrooms rarely live in the hearts of those asked to carry them out. I co-design and co-develop organisational futures — strategies, structures, and frameworks — with the full intelligence of your people, creating strategies that are genuinely owned.

For: NGOs, social enterprises, corporates seeking adaptive strategies

03

System Change & Sector Transformation

The most urgent challenges of our time cannot be solved by any single organisation. I facilitate multi-stakeholder convergences that launch co-created initiatives, align sectors, and build the collective capacity needed to shift entire systems toward social-ecological renewal.

For: Alliances, funders, sector networks, territorial development initiatives

I am a proud practitioner within the global Art of Hosting community — an international network dedicated to participatory leadership and hosting meaningful conversations that matter. Flow Game Host

I am a certified Flow Game host — a profound and playful process that creates the conditions for deep insight, authentic connection, and personal clarity.
